AI for parents

AI for parents should know when not to pretend it knows.

Good AI parenting support combines context, humility, safety boundaries, and practical next steps.

What AI can help with

AI can help parents organize a concern, understand what may be going on, choose a small home action, and prepare for a professional conversation.

What Arbor adds

  • Parent-approved child memory.
  • Non-diagnostic guidance.
  • Daily actions, play, practice, and plans.
  • Professional handoff when needed.

The key test

An AI parenting product should be useful, but also careful. Arbor is built around that boundary.
