Arbor / Professional handoff

Care context

When a child needs a professional, the story should already be clear.

Arbor helps parents turn scattered moments into a concise, parent-approved context packet.

The first appointment problem

Parents often arrive with months of small details in their head. It is hard to explain patterns, attempts, triggers, and progress in a short appointment.

What Arbor can prepare

  • The current concern and relevant history.
  • Patterns over time.
  • Plans, practice, play, and routines already tried at home.
  • Questions for the professional.
  • Only information the parent approves.

Important boundary

Arbor does not replace a professional. It improves the handoff so the professional can begin with better context.
