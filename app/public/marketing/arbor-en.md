# Arbor: Child Development Operating System

Arbor helps parents raise children with more confidence and less guessing. It combines a living child memory, expert developmental guidance, daily rhythm, daily play, personalized story journeys, practice, growth plans, and professional-ready care summaries.

Arbor is not a diagnostic tool, doctor, therapist, or emergency service. It is parent-support software. Parents control what is remembered, deleted, exported, or shared.

## What Arbor Does

- Ask Arbor: guidance connected to the child's age, context, and developmental knowledge.
- My Child: a living child profile of moments, patterns, plans, and outcomes.
- Today and Rhythm: calm, friction, and focus windows with one useful action for today.
- Daily Play: short, stage-aware activities families can do at home.
- Academy: personalized story journeys where the child is the main character.
- Growth Plans and Practice: sleep, language, feelings, transitions, motor skills, and thinking.
- Care and Experts: parent-approved professional summaries for pediatricians, therapists, teachers, and specialists.
