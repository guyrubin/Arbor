# Arbor Data Retention Policy Skeleton

## Retention classes

- Parent-approved child memory: retained until parent deletion, expiry, or account closure policy applies.
- Rejected/deleted memory events: retained as audit metadata where legally allowed.
- Safety review events: retained for audit and incident handling.
- Raw logs: avoid storing raw child-sensitive content in normal application logs.

## Review required

Confirm retention periods with counsel before private beta.
